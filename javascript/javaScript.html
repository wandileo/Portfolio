Super Calculator

The Super‐Calculator gives you the power to glue words together — heck, you can even glue entire sentences — using the concatenation operator. Don’t let all the syllables scare you off. Concatenation is the best invention since socks, and the operator looks exactly like the operator for addition (a plus sign). How simple is that? Simply type in the first word, choose the concatenation operator, type in the second word and — BLAMMO! — you’re concatenating like a pro.
